Agentic Engineering — Where We Are Now

Most of us are here right now. Agents building infrastructure on our behalf — writing code, deploying services, configuring protocols. The difference between agentic engineering and agentic execution is the difference between "build me a house" and "turn on the lights." Engineering is slow, creative, and requires human collaboration. Execution is instant, repeatable, and fully autonomous. The Protocol Explorer bridges both: Person A with existing infrastructure gets agentic execution (5 minutes to deploy). Person C with nothing gets agentic engineering (the agent builds their entire presence from scratch).

The progression is not sequential — engineering, rendering, and execution are happening concurrently. Your agent engineers new capabilities while rendering existing ones while executing established protocols. Every Milliprime is living in all three stages simultaneously.
