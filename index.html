What I Believe
🎯

Design separates winners. In an age of abundance, the cost of goods approaches zero. Design becomes the only scarce resource. Design is how you save money, keep people sane, and achieve what you set out to achieve.

🔄

The unit of competition is the loop. Speed of iteration. Speed of feedback. Speed of learning. Whoever has the tightest loop wins. Not the biggest team. Not the most funding.

🏗️

Build vs. buy flips. If you can build version 1 in hours, the default becomes build. You avoid lock-in, keep velocity, and can commoditize it later instead of paying forever.

🤝

Trust is the economy. We give away everything — complete systems, playbooks, competitive advantages. Creating competitors strengthens the network. The trust chain IS the moat.

🛡️

Virtue and velocity are no longer in conflict. Code scales, humans don't. The most radical thing we can do right now is automate away the gatekeepers and give the tools to the idealists.
